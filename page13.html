What about experience? 

Set Decorator

Furnishes and dresses the sets with appropriate props and set pieces.

Script Supervisor

Keeps track of continuity and notes any deviations from the script during filming.

Makeup and Hair Artist

Designs and applies makeup and hairstyles for actors.

Location Scout

Searches for suitable shooting locations.

Storyboard Artist

Creates visual representations of the script to plan the shots.

Special Effects

Coordinates practical effects.

Choreographer

Plans and coordinates dance or movement sequences if needed.

Social Media Manager

Manages the film's online presence and social media marketing.

Foley Artist

Creates and records additional sound effects.

Casting Director

Helps choose the actors who will play the characters in the film.

Costume Designer

Designs and selects costumes for the characters.

Boom Operator:

Holds and positions the microphone on a boom pole to capture sound.

Craft Services/Catering

Provides food, snacks, and drinks to the cast and crew during production.
