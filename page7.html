Teaching Methods

In my role as an educator, I embrace a philosophy of teaching that empowers students to explore their creativity fearlessly. My teaching methods revolve around exploration and experimentation, project-based learning, and fostering a supportive learning environment.

I believe in personalized learning, tailoring my approach to meet the individual needs of each student. Through regular critique sessions, students gain valuable insights and develop a critical eye for their own work and that of their peers.

Art history and exposure to contemporary artists enrich the curriculum, providing students with a broader context for their artistic exploration. Cultural relevance is at the heart of my teaching, honoring and celebrating the diverse backgrounds and experiences of my students.

Collaboration plays a pivotal role in my teaching style. I encourage teamwork and exchange of ideas through collaborative projects, fostering a sense of community among students.

Ultimately, my teaching philosophy centers on nurturing creativity, fostering personal growth, and preparing students to become confident and passionate artists. I aim to inspire a love for art and storytelling, empowering each student to find their unique artistic voice and express themselves authentically.

Join me on this exciting journey of artistic exploration, where we celebrate the power of creativity and unlock the boundless potential within each individual, embarking on an adventure of self-discovery too create art that speaks to the heart and soul.

Consulting

Story Consulting

Story First






I specialize in helping filmmakers refine their storytelling techniques so helping you develop engaging narratives will be fun. From brainstorming sessions to script analysis, I will assist in shaping plots, character arcs, and thematic elements to help you create captivating and impactful stories.

Tailored Guidance:

As a consultant, I provide one-on-one sessions tailored to the specific needs and goals of each individual project. Whether it's a short film, documentary, or experimental piece, I work closely with filmmakers to understand their vision and offer customized advice to bring it to life effectively.

Feedback

In consulting sessions, I provide honest and detailed critiques, offering praise for strengths and highlighting areas of improvement. This feedback loop is crucial for refining the film and ensuring it reaches its full potential.

Technical Expertise:

With a lifetime experience as a writer, director, and cinematographer gear nerd , I offer valuable insights into best equipment to use, camera techniques, lighting setups, sound recording, and post-production processes. I guide filmmakers in achieving their desired visual and auditory aesthetics by walking them through the gear they need and how to use it.
