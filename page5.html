About me

Born in the U.S. but raised in Ghana, I found solace in the Anansi tales, which shaped my journey back to the U.S. Fueled by the wisdom of these trickster stories, I use filmmaking to share authentic narratives, challenge borders of identity, and inspire fearlessness in embracing our true selves.
